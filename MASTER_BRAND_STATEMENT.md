# Design Orchard LLC — Master Brand Statement

## The Legal Canopy

**Design Orchard LLC** is a Florida-based virtual operating company, publisher, and rights-holding entity for its creative, software, design, and archive branches.

Design Orchard LLC is not a physical place, not the graphic design agency by itself, and not the software studio. It is the legal and operational canopy that allows the ecosystem to function from Florida nationally.

## What Not To Do

```txt
Do not say: Design Orchard is under the Lab.
Say: The Lab is a branch of Design Orchard LLC.
```

```txt
Do not invoice as: DrMarchand’s Lab⚛︎ratory™ alone.
Say: Design Orchard LLC / Branch: DrMarchand’s Lab⚛︎ratory™.
```

```txt
Do not describe: KEJ Studio™ as independent from Design Orchard LLC.
Say: KEJ Studio™ is a branch of Design Orchard LLC.
```

```txt
Do not claim: Design Orchard LLC is a holding company yet.
Say: Design Orchard LLC is a virtual operating company and rights-holding entity.
```
